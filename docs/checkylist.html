This checklist compiles criteria and questions that stem from our reproducibility analysis that systematically analyzed the reproducibility level of 65 papers published at SIGIR during the years 2022-2024. It covers 50 questions based on the broader categorization of Source Code, Datasets, Evaluation, Miscellaneous, and LLMs and Pretrained Models.
